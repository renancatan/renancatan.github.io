Master Data Projects Management

Imagine being able to manage your projects in bulk?



Project objective: The production lines were losing productivity due to masterdata problems. The Masterdata PMO asked for a way to help on the management.

Project development: Using Power BI, the database was simplified and altered in a way that made it simple to see and follow up all the projects at once, allowing to focus only on the problematic ones.

Project result: The impact on the production line went from 0.4% (global productivity) to 0%.
